About

Our Mission

Our major goal is to write free software and to collaborate with student developers, to create a community of excellent programmers. Innovation is what we aim to achieve. We also help student developers to gain an exposure to the competitive world by conducting Hackathons, hands-on workshops, guest lectures and other tech related activities.
The Open Source Community : VIT-AP is led by the faculty coordinator Prof. Asish Kumar Dalai and a team of Sophomores, currently enrolled in the University. The club currently consists of a total of 74 members (both freshers and sophomores).
